# Privacy Policy – Gmail Smart Cleaner

**Effective Date:** February 2026

## Overview

Gmail Smart Cleaner ("Extension") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we access and how we handle it.

## What Data We Access

The Extension only accesses Gmail data **when you explicitly use it**:

- Email metadata (sender, subject, date, labels)
- Gmail labels and folders
- Message IDs for archiving/deleting

## What We Do NOT Do

❌ We **never** store your emails locally
❌ We **never** read email content
❌ We **never** sell or share your data
❌ We **never** track your activity
❌ We **never** auto-delete without your consent

## How We Use Data

Your data is used **only** to:
- Display inbox statistics
- Show top senders
- Execute cleanup actions you request
- Enable undo functionality (temporary)

## Data Storage

- **Access Token:** Stored securely in Chrome's storage (encrypted by Chrome)
- **User Info:** Stored locally (email, name, picture)
- **Undo Data:** Stored temporarily, cleared after 30 seconds
- **No cloud storage or servers**

## Third-Party Services

The Extension uses **official Google APIs**:
- Gmail API for email operations
- OAuth2 for authentication
- No other third-party services

[Google's Privacy Policy applies here](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Your Rights

You can:
- ✓ Logout anytime
- ✓ Revoke permissions in Chrome settings
- ✓ Uninstall the Extension anytime
- ✓ Request data deletion (manual logout clears all)

## Security

- Manifest V3 (latest Chrome security standards)
- OAuth2 secure authentication
- No sensitive data stored permanently
- HTTPS for all API calls

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. You'll be notified of major changes.
